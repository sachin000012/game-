If you start the game, any of the tiles will flicker. You have to click that tile. After that, another tile will flicker. First, you have to click the previous tile, then click the present tile that flickers. It may not flicker only once every time, it can flicker multiple times. You have to remember the pattern. As you go from level to level, the patterns get more complicated and at some point, you're going to screw up and it will tell you that you got it wrong and the game is over. You can press any key to restart the game.
